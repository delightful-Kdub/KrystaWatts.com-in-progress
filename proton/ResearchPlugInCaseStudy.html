User-Driven Design Project

Research and Architecture

The assignment was to conduct user interviews to and contextual research sessions using a task of our own creation. We partnered up to decide on a task for our users and write the interview script or outline. The sessions were conducted individually and then we came back together with our partner to combine findings so our analysis would come from a larger sample set, however the problems we identified and decided to move forward with were done as individuals.

Scenerio and Initial Hypothesis

The task we chose was this scenerio: “Someone close to you has been diagnosed with prostate cancer. You want to act as their advocate and compile a list of treatments to ask the doctor about.”

User Flows

Post-its were used to digitize the recorded sessions.

Affinity Cluster

The Post-its from all 10 user flows were arranged into an affiity cluster. I chose place it on a chart, and change the relative size of each clustering, as a way to visually convey my analysis of the data.
